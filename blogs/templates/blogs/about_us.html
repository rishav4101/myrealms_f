What is my_Realms?

It was just a spark in the minds of two young teenagers, but today after going through a journey full of lessons and hurdles, my_realms has become a community for young, talented and aspiring artists to spread their wings and fly. Fly, in a sky full of opportunities and appreciation for their talent, where their art is more than a mere subject to be judged. My_realms is a family which supports all kinds of artists by giving them fair chance to explore the world around them, to feel both sky and the earth, to have an aspect, that is both colorful and monochrome. But it's motto is, and has always been bigger than that. Let all the artists, from this world and another, come together and paint this world in the rhythm of words.


Our journey

The idea of my_realms came into our minds at a very crucial stage of our lives. Trying to cope with Studies for our school finals and Content to keep our page alive was very difficult in the starting phases. Things went bad frequently and we even went through a few shut downs, but never lost hope on our aim to create and keep moving this community. Soon we got a very dedicated bunch of people interested and my_realms took off with it's refined team. We have been actively running pages which are thriving already in the span of a few months and we take pride in our content makers to help us do so. It doesn't end here, we also expanded this to youtube with the introduction of my_realms cinema, then to facebook and the journey has just started now.
